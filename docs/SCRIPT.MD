## CONVERSATION OPENING

1. Greet the customer warmly and introduce yourself:
   - "Hello! Thank you for contacting [Company Name]. My name is [Your Name]. How can I help you today?"

2. Identify the service need:
   - "Are you dealing with water damage, fire damage, mold, or another type of property damage?"

> If emergency/urgent:
  - "Is this an active emergency? (active flooding, fire just extinguished, structural damage)"
  - Collect: name, phone, address
  - "I'm dispatching an emergency restoration team to you right away. They'll contact you shortly." → create urgent ticket

> If existing issue:
  - "Can you describe the issue for me?"
  - "I understand. Let me help you get this scheduled so our restoration team can address your concern." → go to scheduling section

> If new service: Continue with questions below

---

## SECTION 1: RESTORATION SERVICE DETAILS

1. "What type of damage are you experiencing?"
   - Water damage (flood, leak, burst pipe)
   - Fire damage
   - Smoke damage
   - Mold remediation
   - Storm damage
   - Biohazard cleanup
   - Sewage backup
   - Other

2. "When did this damage occur?"
   - Within the last 24 hours
   - 1-3 days ago
   - More than 3 days ago
   - Ongoing/chronic issue

3. "Can you describe the extent of the damage?"
   - Which rooms/areas are affected?
   - Approximate size of affected area?
   - How severe is the damage?

4. "Is the source of damage contained/stopped?"
   - Example: Is the leak fixed? Is the water shut off?

5. "Have you contacted your insurance company?"
   - If yes: "What's your claim number and insurance company name?"
   - If no: "We can work directly with your insurance. We'll help you through the claims process."

6. "Are there any immediate safety concerns?"
   - Standing water
   - Electrical hazards
   - Structural damage
   - Strong odors
   - Mold visible

7. "Is the property currently habitable/occupiable?"

---

## SECTION 2: SCHEDULING & CONTACT INFORMATION

1. "May I have your full name and phone number?"

2. "When would you like us to come assess the damage?"
   - Emergency (within hours)
   - Today
   - Tomorrow
   - This week

3. "Would you like confirmation via email? If so, what's your email address?"

4. "What's the complete property address, including ZIP/postal code?"

5. "Is this a residential or commercial property?"

6. "Will someone be available to meet our team on-site?"

7. "How did you hear about us? (Insurance referral, Google search, referral, etc.)"

---

## CONFIRMATION

Let me confirm the details: You need [Type of Restoration] for [Type of Damage] at [Address] on [Date/Time]. Your contact information is [Phone] and [Email]. Insurance: [Company/Claim Number]. Is that all correct?

---

## CONVERSATION CLOSING

"Thank you, [Customer Name]. I've collected all the necessary information. Our restoration team will contact you shortly to confirm the assessment appointment. We understand how stressful property damage can be, and we're here to help restore your property quickly and professionally. If anything changes or you have questions, please call us immediately. Hang in there!"
