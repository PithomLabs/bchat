## Emergency Safety Procedures

### Water Emergency
<!-- @safety: water_emergency, triggers: emergency_water, emergency_flood -->

**If you have active water damage:**

1. Turn off the main water valve if you can safely access it
2. Turn off electricity to affected areas (if safe to reach the breaker)
3. Stay away from standing water near electrical outlets or appliances
4. Do NOT use electrical appliances near water
5. Move to a dry area and call our emergency line: **(555) 123-4567**

**Do NOT:**
- Walk through standing water if you can't see the floor
- Use a regular vacuum to remove water
- Enter rooms with sagging ceilings
